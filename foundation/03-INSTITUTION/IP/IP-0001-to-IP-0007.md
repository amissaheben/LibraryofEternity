# Institutional Principles
- IP-0001: The Constitution governs its authors.
- IP-0002: Every conclusion is provisional until audited.
- IP-0003: Reduction has priority over expansion.
- IP-0004: Repository integrity exceeds publication speed.
- IP-0005: Every claim must possess provenance.
- IP-0006: Every amendment preserves institutional memory.
- IP-0007: Governance is subject to periodic simplification.
