# LAW-0002 — The Law of Definition

## Constitutional Definition

The constitutional determination of the limits within which an identity remains itself and beyond which it is another.

## Constitutional Maxim

> Nothing can be known unless something is bounded.

## Ontological Function

Provides the irreducible constitutional capability of **Boundedness**.

## Immediate Constitutional Consequence

**Boundedness:** The unique capability contributed by this Law to the Minimal Capability Basis.

## Derived Constitutional Contributions

- Definition + Connection → Relational Space
- Definition + Selection → Stable Possibility
- Definition + Resonance → Persistent Boundary

## Constitutional Invariants

- Definition cannot create identity.
- Definition cannot replace identity.
- Definition cannot eliminate distinction while remaining definition.

## Jurisdiction

This Law governs its named constitutional capability only. It must not be used as a substitute for any later measure, structure, threshold, interpretation, or institutional doctrine.
