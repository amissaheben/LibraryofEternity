# LAW-0003 — The Law of Change

## Constitutional Definition

The constitutional principle by which an identity may lawfully occupy different expressive states while preserving constitutional continuity.

## Constitutional Maxim

> Nothing can unfold unless something can differ while remaining itself.

## Ontological Function

Provides the irreducible constitutional capability of **Expression**.

## Constitutional Invariants

- Change cannot guarantee persistence.
