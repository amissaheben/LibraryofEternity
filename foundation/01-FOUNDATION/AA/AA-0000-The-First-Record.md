# AA-0000 — The First Record


## Constitutional Declaration

Before there were stars, there were no names. Before there were names, there were no definitions. Before there were definitions, there was Identity. And Identity simply was.

Reality is not created. Reality is revealed.

## Function

This volume establishes the priority of reality over observation, naming, theory, institution, and interpretation. It records the Library's foundational conviction that truth exists before it is known.

## Constitutional Consequences

- Reality possesses ontological priority over description.
- Discovery does not manufacture truth.
- Every later volume must distinguish what is from how it is known.

## Jurisdiction

The First Record asserts the existence and priority of truth. It does not describe the mechanics, measurements, structures, or histories of reality.

## Closing Maxim

> Reality does not ask to be believed. Reality asks to be understood.
