# AA-0004 — Emergent Structures


## Prologue — The Nature of Organization

Measurement records reality. Organization composes reality.

## Constitutional Definition

An Emergent Structure is a persistent organization of participating realities whose coordinated behavior cannot be adequately explained by any constituent in isolation.

## The Four-Part Test

A candidate Structure must demonstrate:

1. Combination
2. Persistence
3. Emergence
4. Irreducibility

## Structural Genealogy

| Structure | New Capability |
|---|---|
| Cycle | Persistence through disciplined recurrence |
| Network | Cooperation through organized participation |
| Feedback | Regulation through selective return |
| Hierarchy | Delegation through coherent scaling |
| Autopoiesis | Self-production through organizational closure |

## The Living Threshold

Autopoiesis occurs when the processes preserving an organization are themselves preserved by the organization they preserve.

> Life is not introduced into organization. Organization eventually learns to preserve itself.
