# AA-0001 — The Nature of Reality


## Purpose

To define the minimum conditions under which anything may be recognized as real within the Infinite Weave.

## The Three Conditions of Existence

### Identity
A reality must possess an intrinsic nature by which it is itself.

### Definition
A reality must possess limits through which it is distinguishable.

### Participation
A reality must enter lawful relation within the Weave and contribute to a coherent whole.

## The Coherence Doctrine

> Identity + Definition + Participation = Coherence.

Coherence is not imposed by an observer. It is the systemic consistency through which a reality remains identifiable, bounded, and participatory.

## Constitutional Definition of Reality

> To be real is to be identifiable, definable, and participatory.

## Epistemological Note

Reality remains real whether or not it is understood. Understanding is the disciplined alignment of description with the identity and structure already present.

## Closing Reflection

> Reality does not ask to be believed. Reality asks to be understood.
